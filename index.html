About Me

My name is Candra — friends call me Candy. Last name's Fried (pronounced | Freed). I'm a UIX Design Engineer, Developer & Visual Artist based in Desoto, TX. I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas and moved to Philadelphia many moons ago for school. I attended Moore College of Art and Design where I majored in Illustration and Graphic Design. In 2014, I earned my Masters in Computer Information Science at Penn State, Great Valley. I’m currently continuing my education in the Southern Methodist University (SMU) Full Stack Development Certification Program.

I specialize in a variety of creative services ranging from professional illustrations, print-work to web development. I'm proficient in the full Adobe Creative Suite, HTML 5, CSS, Javascript and several other graphic programs, applications and frameworks.
